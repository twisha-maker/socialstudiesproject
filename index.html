1. demonetization-




demonetization means the withdrawal of a coin, note, or precious metal from use as legal tender. In 2016, the Indian government decided to demonetize the 500- and 1000- rupee notes, the two biggest denominations in its currency system.Demonetization took place in India to push people to pay taxes,make India a digital country and so on.One of its major reason is that the money used mostly in India before Demonetization was notes of RS500 and Rs 1000 and all the black money was stored using these notes.After demoneization theese notes were not of any use so the use of black money was less encouraged.
